The Roll Call System

Roll Call is a personal class management system that alleviates the tedious work of administrative tasks outside of the classroom. It provides virtually effortless attendance and roll reporting.

Get in Touch

The Problem

At EdTech Solutions we understand that there is no standard attendance taking mechanism at colleges and universities in the United States, and attendance currently recorded and maintained manually by the professor of each class. This is a similar problem for many colleges and universities so care must be taken to ensure the integrity, accuracy, and availability of attendance records each semester.

We also understand that a major issue with students  college is their attendance in class. Many students do not attend class regularly and, as a result, receive low grades or fail. This seriously affects the persistence and progression of students. Many classes in the core education of STEM programs are large, over 50 but less than 150. Professors are responsible for recording the attendance of students at each meeting. Some actually call the roll orally, but most with large classes have the students sign-in on a roll sheet, which is analyzed at a later date. However, professors must spend time entering data in a roll book or spreadsheet manually during the semester.
